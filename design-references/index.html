Design Variations

Six neumorphic treatments of the same LIFEX OS landing page. Click any tile to open the variation in a new tab.

Baseline
Variation 01
Classic Dark

Pure dark neumorphism. All interactive elements use raised/inset/pressed shadow recipes. No glass, no glow, no starfield. Clean and minimal — the reference design.

Open variation →
Variation 02
Glass Neumorphism

Module and pricing cards gain frosted glass (backdrop-blur 12px). Nav becomes glassmorphic on scroll. Hover reveals a brand-colour inner glow on each card.

Open variation →
Variation 03
Ambient Glow

Each module card has a per-brand radial glow orb above it. Status chips gain outer glow. Hero H1 flows orange→violet→blue. CTA pulses with accent glow on hover.

Open variation →
Variation 04
Starfield Depth

A pure CSS starfield of 120 dots sits fixed behind everything. A subset of 40 dots twinkle on a 10s cycle. Deeper shadows and a faint violet ambient behind the hero H1.

Open variation →
Variation 05
Light Mode

Day-mode neumorphism: bg flips to #e4e7ef, shadows use blue-grey dark and pure white light for the pillowy effect. Status chips get light-mode border treatments.

Open variation →
User's pick
Variation 06
v1 + Starfield

Variation 1 with one addition only: a subtle CSS starfield. 50 static stars + 30 twinkling on a 6s cycle. Every other token, shadow, color, and layout is byte-for-byte identical to the baseline.
